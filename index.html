The Process

Your Lip Blushing Journey

A comfortable, transparent experience from first message to final reveal.

01

Free Consultation

We discuss your goals, skin tone, and desired colour palette. Patch test is conducted if needed.

02

Shape Design

We map your perfect lip shape together, correcting asymmetry and enhancing definition before a single pigment is applied.

03

Numbing & Comfort

A professional-grade topical numbing cream is applied for 20–30 minutes to ensure a virtually painless experience.

04

Pigment Application

Using Korean micro-needling technique, colour is delicately layered into the lips for the most natural, dimensional effect.

05

Healing & Aftercare

We walk you through simple 7-day aftercare. Lips fully settle in 4–6 weeks revealing your gorgeous final colour.

06

Touch-Up Session

A complimentary touch-up is scheduled 6–8 weeks later to perfect the colour and ensure longevity.
